Welcome to Wellness Website

Build soothing digital health tools with us

We are a digital health team creating accessible, low-stress experiences for the community, those who cannot afford healthcare and clinicians. We are looking for people who are passionate about providing next gen health tech, and care about thoughful and inclusive designs.

Our Mission

We at Wellness are commited to ensuring that all members of our community enjoy a caring and supportive journey to better health.

Our Values

  • Empathy: We prioritize understanding and compassion in all our interactions, ensuring that our products and services truly meet the needs of our users.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and open communication, fostering a culture where diverse perspectives are valued and contribute to our success.
  • Innovation: We are committed to continuous learning and improvement, embracing new ideas and technologies to create cutting-edge solutions for healthcare.
  • Inclusivity: We strive to create an environment where everyone feels welcome and valued, promoting diversity and accessibility in all aspects of our work.

Our Culture

At Wellness, we foster a culture of creativity, collaboration and continuous learning. We believe in creating a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone can thrive and contribute to our mission of improving healthcare for all.

Our Vision

We envision a future where healthcare is accessible, personalized and empowering for everyone. We strive to create innovative digital health solutions that enhance the well-being of individuals and communities worldwide, and pave the way for the next generation of the health industry.

Teams and Collaboration sessions

You will be working in small, cross-functional groups that meet regularly in calm, focused sessions. These "class times" are when teams co-design, test ideas and review work together.

  • Care Navigation Team: Helps in finding the right support at the right time


    • Tuesday, 10:30 AM - Screen reader testing
    • Thursday, 3:00 PM- Colour contrast and content audit

  • Accesibility Team: Ensures our products are usable with assistive technologies and follow strong accessibility practices.


    • Monday, 9:00 AM - Patient onboarding review
    • Wednesday, 1:30 PM - Support flow workshop

  • Clinical Content Team: Focuses on creating clear, concise and empathetic content for patients


    • Monday, 11:00 AM - Content review and feedback
    • Friday, 2:00 PM - User testing with patients
